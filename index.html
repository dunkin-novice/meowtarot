Tarot Reading FAQ

Is MeowTarot free?

Yes. You can use the daily tarot, full 3-card readings, and tarot card meanings for free.

What can I ask the cards?

Most users ask about love, career, money, and life direction. We encourage questions that focus on clarity and choices, not fear.

Is this fortune-telling or guidance?

We treat tarot as a tool for reflection and emotional insight, not fixed fate. Always use your own judgment and, when needed, consult professionals.

Is it safe for my mental health?

We aim for psychologically safe language. Tarot is for reflection, not a substitute for therapy or professional advice.
