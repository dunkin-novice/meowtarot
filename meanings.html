    Tarot card meanings FAQs

    Quick answers to common questions about reading and applying tarot interpretations.

    What are tarot card meanings?

    They are the symbols, archetypes, and guidance behind each card in upright and reversed positions.

    How do upright and reversed meanings differ?

    Upright meanings highlight the main supportive energy; reversed meanings reveal blocks, delays, or hidden lessons.

    Can I use these meanings for love, career, and money?

    Yes. MeowTarot offers prompts you can map directly to relationships, work, and finances.

    How do I find a specific tarot card?

    Use the search bar or quick chips to jump to cards like The Fool, The Lovers, Death, or Ace of Cups.

    Do I need experience to read tarot meanings?

    No. Start with the learning helpers and build confidence one card at a time.
