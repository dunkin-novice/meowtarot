Core Meaning

difficult choice, blocked emotions, indecision, stalemate, negotiation

Reading Summary

Past hesitation has led to present stalemate. Soon, however, you must make a decisive choice to unlock the situation and move into the future.

Light & Shadow Keywords

Light: balance, truce, crossroads, decisions, peace

Shadow: indecision, confusion, avoidance, stalemate, blocked emotions
