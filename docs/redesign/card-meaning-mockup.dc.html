Quick answers

What is the core meaning of The Fool?

New Beginnings, Spontaneity, Leap of Faith, Innocence, Limitless Potential.

What does The Fool suggest in a reading?

Bold choices from your past have led you to a precipice where trust in destiny is required once more. The gates of possibility are swinging open, inviting you to explore.

What are the key themes of The Fool?

Light: Innocence, New beginnings, Freedom, Adventure, Spontaneity, Trust. Shadow: Recklessness, Naivety, Foolishness, Risk-taking, Distraction.

What does The Fool mean in love?

In love readings, The Fool points to fresh starts, honest curiosity, and taking a heartfelt risk. For singles, it can signal exciting new connections; for couples, it invites trying a new chapter with openness and trust.
